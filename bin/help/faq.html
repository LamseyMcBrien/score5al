Is Score5al free software? Can I redistribute it?
Score5al is not currently offered under a commercial license. The author, Liam McBrien, reserves all rights with regards to the usage and distribution of the software; please contact him for permission to use Score5al or share it with others.

How does the match scoring work in Sur5al/Score5al?
Please see the "Scoring" section on the introduction page.

How do I apply a match points penalty/bonus to a team?
Use the "Points Adjustment" controls in the Teams tab.

I'm having trouble generating a perfect match schedule.
Score5al's auto-assignment function does the best job it can, but depending on how many teams, heats and jams you have, and especially how many teams can be in each heat, this might not be possible. You may need to tweak your match parameters.

How can I visualise the distribution of teams to heats?
Use the Show Distribution function from the "Match" menu.

Can I export my match data to use it outside Score5al?
Yes, you can export all match data from Score5al to a CSV file, as described on the Controls page.

How do I get the scoreboard out of full-screen mode?
If the scoreboard is in full-screen mode and blocking your access to the main window's Scoreboard menu, you can press the Escape key to get out of full-screen mode.

I'm having trouble getting the scoreboard displaying properly.
Check out the Hints and Tips section of the Scoreboard page.
